Biography

I am a 4th-year (2021-now) Ph.D. student at MSC Lab in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of California at Berkeley, under the co-supervision of Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka and Prof. David Bogy.

My research interest includes diffusion models and autonomous driving.
